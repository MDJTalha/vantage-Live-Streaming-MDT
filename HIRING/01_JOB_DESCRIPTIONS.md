## 1. Executive Leadership

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

**Location:** Remote (US/EU) or San Francisco, CA  
**Reports to:** CEO  
**Compensation:** $400,000 - $600,000 base + 0.5-2.0% equity + benefits  
**Team Size:** 84 direct + indirect reports  

---

#### About VANTAGE

VANTAGE is transforming enterprise live streaming and video conferencing. We're building the most advanced, secure, and intelligent platform for Fortune 500 companies, board meetings, and executive leadership teams. Our mission is to become the #1 enterprise video platform by 2028.

We're backed by top-tier investors and growing rapidly. Our platform supports 100+ concurrent participants with HD video, real-time collaboration, AI-powered insights, and end-to-end encryption.

#### The Opportunity

As CTO, you'll lead our technical vision and engineering organization during the most critical phase of our growth. You'll report directly to the CEO and have full ownership of:

- Technical strategy and architecture for breakthrough features
- Building and scaling the engineering organization (68 engineers across 10 teams)
- Security and compliance (SOC 2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP, HIPAA)
- AI/ML strategy including our Neural Meeting Engine
- Infrastructure for 99.99% uptime and global scale

This is a rare opportunity to build a category-defining platform from the ground up while competing with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Webex.

#### What You'll Do

**Technical Leadership (40%)**
- Define and execute the 18-month technical roadmap
- Make critical architecture decisions for microservices, AI infrastructure, and media quality
- Drive breakthrough feature development (holographic presence, universal translation, predictive AI)
- Establish engineering culture, values, and best practices
- Represent VANTAGE at technical conferences and with enterprise customers

**Organization Building (35%)**
- Hire and develop VP-level leadership (VP Engineering, VP AI/ML, VP Security)
- Scale engineering from ~10 to 68+ engineers in 18 months
- Build diverse, high-performing teams across platform, media, AI, security, and DevOps
- Create career ladders, leveling frameworks, and promotion processes
- Establish remote-first engineering culture

**Execution & Delivery (25%)**
- Implement agile processes with 2-week sprints and quarterly OKRs
- Fix critical security vulnerabilities (C-01 through C-05) in first 60 days
- Deliver enterprise certifications (SOC 2 Type II in 9 months)
- Achieve 99.99% uptime SLA by Month 12
- Launch breakthrough AI features by Month 12

#### What We're Looking For

**Required Experience**
- 15+ years of software engineering experience
- 5+ years as CTO, VP Engineering, or equivalent at a video/streaming company
- Proven track record scaling platforms to 10M+ users
- Deep expertise in WebRTC, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure
- Experience managing 50+ person engineering organizations
- Strong understanding of AI/ML infrastructure and production ML

**Preferred Background**
- Previous experience at Zoom, Webex, Teams, Twitch, Agora, or similar
- Built and scaled microservices architecture
- Led SOC 2, ISO 27001, or FedRAMP certification processes
- Experience with real-time media processing at scale
- Track record of successful exits (IPO or acquisition)

**Leadership Qualities**
- Visionary thinker with hands-on execution mindset
- Exceptional communicator (written, verbal, technical, business)
- Customer-obsessed with deep empathy for enterprise buyers
- Builder of people - develops leaders, not just individual contributors
- Thrives in high-growth, ambiguous environments

#### Technical Skills

**Must Have**
- Architecture: Microservices, event-driven systems, API design
- Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, AWS/GCP, Terraform
- Databases: PostgreSQL, Redis, event streaming (Kafka)
- Languages: Node.js, TypeScript, Python, Go
- WebRTC: SFU architecture, simulcast, adaptive bitrate
- Security: E2EE, encryption, authentication, compliance

**Nice to Have**
- AI/ML: LLMs, transformers, Whisper, production ML pipelines
- Media: Video codecs (H.264, VP9, AV1), audio codecs (Opus)
- Real-time: WebSocket, Socket.IO, MQTT
- Observability: Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog, distributed tracing

#### Our Stack

- **Backend:** Node.js, TypeScript, Express, Prisma, PostgreSQL, Redis
- **Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, WebRTC
- **Media:** Mediasoup, WebRTC, Coturn (STUN/TURN)
- **AI:** PyTorch, Whisper, Transformers, GPU clusters (A100)
- **Infrastructure:** Kubernetes, Docker, AWS, Terraform, Cloudflare
- **Monitoring:** Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog, Sentry

#### Benefits

- **Equity:** 0.5-2.0% of fully diluted shares (4-year vesting, 1-year cliff)
- **Health:** 100% medical, dental, vision coverage (employee + family)
- **401(k):** 4% company match
- **PTO:** Unlimited paid time off
- **Parental Leave:** 16 weeks paid (primary), 8 weeks (secondary)
- **Learning:** $10,000/year budget for conferences, courses, coaching
- **Home Office:** $5,000 setup stipend + $200/month remote allowance
- **Travel:** Quarterly team offsites (all expenses paid)

#### Interview Process

1. **Initial Conversation** (30 min) - CEO/Founder
2. **Technical Deep Dive** (90 min) - Engineering leadership
3. **Architecture Exercise** (2 hours) - Present technical vision
4. **Leadership Panel** (60 min) - VP-level interviews
5. **CEO Final** (60 min) - Vision, culture, compensation
6. **Reference Checks** - 3-5 professional references

**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks from first interview to offer
